# Module 1 Check
## What you should be able to do now
- Read a price chart and name the parts of a candlestick.
- Identify a trading range on a daily chart.
- Draw support and resistance lines on a current chart.
- Tell a breakout from a fakeout with simple criteria.
## Quick questions
- What makes a candle body different from a wick?
- Why is a tested level stronger than an untested one?
- What does it mean when price breaks a range and then returns inside it?
## Chart task
On a recent NVDA chart, label three candles, draw the current range, add S/R lines, and point out one breakout and one fakeout.
## Before you move on
If you cannot explain why a move was a fakeout instead of a breakout, revisit the support and resistance lesson.
