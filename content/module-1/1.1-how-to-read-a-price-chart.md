# How to read a price chart
## The idea in one sentence
You can learn the story behind the lines and bars on a chart.
## Why this matters
Charts are the simplest way to see what price has done and what people are paying.
## How it works
A price chart records the price at every moment and turns the data into visible patterns.
> Key concept: A chart is a record of past buying and selling, not a prediction of the future.
## What this looks like in the market
On NVDA, each daily candlestick shows the open, high, low, and close for one trading day.
## Common mistake
The biggest mistake is reading the chart like a crystal ball instead of a record of order flow.
