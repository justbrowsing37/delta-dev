# Activity 1.1A — Label open/close/high/low on 3 candles of NVDA daily
## What you're doing
You are practicing reading the basic parts of a candlestick.
## What you need
A daily NVDA price chart and a way to mark the candle components.
## Your task
Pick three daily candles and label the open, close, high, and low on each one.
## What to look for
Notice how the open and close define the body and how the wicks show the day's extremes.
