# What is a trading range?
## The idea in one sentence
A trading range is a price area where buyers and sellers are roughly balanced.
## Why this matters
Ranges tell you when the market is pausing and where the next move may begin.
## How it works
Price moves back and forth between support and resistance until one side wins.
> Key concept: A range is a market pause, not a guarantee that price will stay there forever.
## What this looks like in the market
NVDA can trade sideways for days or weeks inside a horizontal band before breaking out.
## Common mistake
People often think a range means the stock is boring; it actually shows where supply and demand are nearby.
