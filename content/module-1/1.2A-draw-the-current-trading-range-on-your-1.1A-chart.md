# Activity 1.2A — Draw the current trading range on your 1.1A chart
## What you're doing
You are turning raw candlesticks into a visual range.
## What you need
Your annotated NVDA daily chart from 1.1A and a horizontal line tool.
## Your task
Identify the current range high and low, then draw the range on the chart.
## What to look for
Find areas where price turned repeatedly and where the next break would matter.
