# Support and resistance
## The idea in one sentence
Support and resistance are price levels where buyers or sellers pause the move.
## Why this matters
These levels help you see where the market may stall or reverse.
## How it works
Support forms when buyers step in near a prior low; resistance forms when sellers step in near a prior high.
> Key concept: Levels become more meaningful when price has tested them multiple times.
## What this looks like in the market
A tested NVDA swing low can act as support while a tested swing high can act as resistance.
## Common mistake
Treating every high or low as a strong level instead of focusing on tested levels.
