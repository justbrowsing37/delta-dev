# Breakouts vs fakeouts
## The idea in one sentence
Not every move out of a range is a true breakout.
## Why this matters
Telling the difference helps you avoid false signals and stay with the real move.
## How it works
A breakout is confirmed when price follows through; a fakeout quickly reverses back inside the range.
> Key concept: Volume, follow-through, and context matter more than the first candle.
## What this looks like in the market
NVDA may spike above a ceiling and then fall back inside the range, creating a fakeout.
## Common mistake
Buying the first move out of a range before the breakout has real follow-through.
