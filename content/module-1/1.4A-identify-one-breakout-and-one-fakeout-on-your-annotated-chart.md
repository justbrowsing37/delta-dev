# Activity 1.4A — Identify one breakout and one fakeout on your annotated chart
## What you're doing
You are practicing the key difference between a real range breakout and a trap.
## What you need
Your annotated NVDA chart with range and S/R levels.
## Your task
Pick one example of a breakout and one example of a fakeout from recent price action.
## What to look for
Compare the follow-through after the first move and whether price returned back inside the range.
