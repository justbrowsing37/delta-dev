- Explain the difference between retail and institutional trading approaches.
- Identify liquidity clusters and stop hunt setups.
- Recognize a sweep versus a genuine breakout.
- Read a 4H range and place your chart position within it.

- What role does liquidity play in a stop hunt?
- How does a 4H range differ from a daily range?
- Why is a genuine breakout more than just a break of a line?
