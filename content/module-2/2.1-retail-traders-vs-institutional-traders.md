# Retail traders vs institutional traders
## The idea in one sentence
Retail traders and institutional traders often think about price differently.
## Why this matters
Understanding that difference helps you see why large moves happen and where liquidity lives.
## How it works
Institutions move bigger size and often hunt liquidity, while retail traders often trade obvious levels.
> Key concept: Volume and order intent are not the same at retail speed versus institutional scale.
## What this looks like in the market
A retail breakout can fail when a large institutional seller absorbs the move.
## Common mistake
Treating all market participants as the same instead of respecting different timeframes and size.
