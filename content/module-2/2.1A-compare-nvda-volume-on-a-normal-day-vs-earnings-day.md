# Activity 2.1A — Compare NVDA volume on a normal day vs earnings day
## What you're doing
You are comparing how volume changes when the market reacts to news.
## What you need
A NVDA daily chart with volume and a normal day plus an earnings day.
## Your task
Find one normal trading day and one earnings day, then compare the volume bars and price behavior.
## What to look for
Notice how much larger the volume is on the news day and whether price made a bigger move.
