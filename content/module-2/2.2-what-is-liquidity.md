# What is liquidity?
## The idea in one sentence
Liquidity is how easily a trade can happen without moving the price too much.
## Why this matters
Markets with more liquidity are easier to read and harder to move with a small order.
## How it works
When many orders are available near the current price, liquidity is high; when orders thin out, liquidity is low.
> Key concept: Liquidity is a property of the market, not a direction.
## What this looks like in the market
A liquid NVDA stock may trade through a level with steady candles, while low liquidity can cause choppy swings.
## Common mistake
Confusing volatility with liquidity; they are related but not identical.
