# Activity 2.3A — Find a stop hunt candle — mark stops, sweep, reversal
## What you're doing
You are turning a real move into a story of liquidity and reaction.
## What you need
A recent chart with a clear sweep and reversal.
## Your task
Pick one candle or move, label where stops were likely hit, where the sweep occurred, and where the reversal began.
## What to look for
Look for a decisive move through a level followed by a quick reversal back above or below it.
