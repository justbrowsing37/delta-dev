# Reading the 4H range
## The idea in one sentence
The 4H chart shows a broader range that can guide swing and intraday decisions.
## Why this matters
The 4H range gives you a larger view of structure and the context for daily moves.
## How it works
Higher timeframes often contain the moves seen on lower timeframes and can expose stronger levels.
> Key concept: A 4H range is a bigger version of the daily range, with more weight behind its levels.
## What this looks like in the market
On NVDA, a 4H range may span several days and define the next important breakout area.
## Common mistake
Using only the daily chart and missing the larger 4H framework.
