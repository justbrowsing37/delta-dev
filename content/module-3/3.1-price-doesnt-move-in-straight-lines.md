# Price doesn't move in straight lines
## The idea in one sentence
Price path is rarely smooth; it moves in waves and pauses.
## Why this matters
Understanding the jagged nature of price helps you avoid expecting a straight trend.
## How it works
Price often overshoots, pulls back, and pauses before continuing in a direction.
> Key concept: Market movement is a series of smaller moves within larger moves.
## What this looks like in the market
NVDA can rally, pull back, rally again, and still be in the same overall trend.
## Common mistake
Seeing every pullback as a new reversal instead of part of the same move.
