# Activity 3.1A — Trace a real move — find where it looked wrong before it went right
## What you're doing
You are following price through a confusing sequence of swings.
## What you need
A chart of a real NVDA move and a way to mark the phases.
## Your task
Choose one move and mark the start, the misleading part, and the eventual correct direction.
## What to look for
Look for a pullback or pause that made the move look like it had failed before it continued.
## Connects to
3.1 Price doesn't move in straight lines
