# Activity 3.2A — Mark the obvious levels on a chart — observe where price visited
## What you're doing
You are mapping the levels most traders are watching.
## What you need
A chart and a way to highlight obvious highs and lows.
## Your task
Identify the key levels and note how price came back to them.
## What to look for
Observe whether price paused, reversed, or pulled back at these levels.
