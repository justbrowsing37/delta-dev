# The engineered move
## The idea in one sentence
Some price moves are designed to create the conditions needed for the next leg.
## Why this matters
This helps you see why a move that looks random may actually be deliberate.
## How it works
The market can test a level, sweep liquidity, and then use the cleared order flow to move in the intended direction.
> Key concept: An engineered move is a sequence, not a single candle.
## What this looks like in the market
Price extends beyond a level, traps weak hands, and then accelerates the other way.
## Common mistake
Calling the first extension the trade instead of understanding the full engineered move.
