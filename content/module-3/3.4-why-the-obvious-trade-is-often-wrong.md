# Why the obvious trade is often wrong
## The idea in one sentence
The most obvious setup is often the one that the market uses to trap the most people.
## Why this matters
Recognizing traps helps you avoid overconfidence and premature entries.
## How it works
When too many people line up on the same side, the market can reverse against the obvious position.
> Key concept: The obvious trade is often crowded, which makes it a high-risk setup.
## What this looks like in the market
A breakout that looks perfect can turn into a fakeout if everyone is on the same side.
## Common mistake
Jumping into the setup just because it looks textbook without considering the other side.
