# Activity 3.4A — Three examples — identify which side got trapped and why
## What you're doing
You are practicing the mental discipline of the non-obvious read.
## What you need
Three chart examples with obvious-looking trades.
## Your task
For each example, write which side was likely trapped and why.
## What to look for
Find where price moved to the obvious level, then turned against the majority.

3.4 Why the obvious trade is often wrong
