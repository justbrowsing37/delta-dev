# Module 4 Check
## What you should be able to do now
- Recognize ranges, breakouts, sweeps, and trend structure visually.
- Know the difference between a breakout and a false breakout.
- Identify when a wick is a sweep versus ordinary noise.
- Map a trend shift and describe the structure change.
## Quick questions
- What separates a range from a trend?
- When is a wick more likely a sweep than a rejection?
- How do you know a structural shift has happened?
## Chart task
Use a current chart to label a range, identify a false breakout, find a wick sweep, and map a trend shift.
## Before you move on
If you cannot identify the shift point in a trend, review the trend structure lesson.
