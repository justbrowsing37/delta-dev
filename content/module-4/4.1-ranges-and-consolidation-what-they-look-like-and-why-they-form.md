# Ranges and consolidation — what they look like and why they form
## The idea in one sentence
A range is a pause in the market where buyers and sellers are in balance.
## Why this matters
Recognizing consolidation helps you avoid trading on noise.
## How it works
Price moves into a tighter area after a trend or before the next significant move.
> Key concept: Consolidation is often the market resting before a new decision.
## What this looks like in the market
NVDA can trade sideways in a narrow band before breaking higher or lower.
## Common mistake
Confusing range trading with chaos when it is actually an important market state.
