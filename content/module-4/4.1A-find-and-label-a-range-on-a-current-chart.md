# Activity 4.1A — Find and label a range on a current chart
## What you're doing
You are turning a period of sideways price action into a clear range.
## What you need
A current NVDA chart and line drawing tools.
## Your task
Identify a period of consolidation and mark the range boundaries.
## What to look for
Choose the area where price moved between two levels without making a clear trend.
