# Breakouts and false breakouts — visual differences and timeframe context
## The idea in one sentence
A true breakout behaves differently from a false breakout when viewed in context.
## Why this matters
Seeing the difference helps you avoid traps and false signals.
## How it works
A real breakout shows follow-through and range expansion; a false breakout fails and returns inside the range.
> Key concept: Timeframe context changes how much weight you give a breakout.
## What this looks like in the market
A false breakout on a 15-minute chart may still be noise on a daily chart.
## Common mistake
Treating a single candle on a low timeframe as a confirmed breakout.
