# Activity 4.2A — Spot the false break — find one on NVDA or META
## What you're doing
You are practicing the visual skill of spotting a failed breakout.
## What you need
A chart of NVDA or META with a recent breakout attempt.
## Your task
Identify one false breakout and explain why it failed.
## What to look for
Look for price moving above a level and then quickly returning below it.

4.2 Breakouts and false breakouts
