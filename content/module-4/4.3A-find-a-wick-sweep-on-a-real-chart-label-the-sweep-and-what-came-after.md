# Activity 4.3A — Find a wick sweep on a real chart — label the sweep and what came after
## What you're doing
You are practicing recognition of a sweep and the following reaction.
## What you need
A chart with a clear wick sweep.
## Your task
Mark the sweep candle and describe the move that followed.
## What to look for
Notice whether price reversed, paused, or continued after the sweep.
