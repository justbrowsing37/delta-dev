# Trend structure — higher highs, lower lows, and structural shifts
## The idea in one sentence
Trend structure is the pattern of higher highs and higher lows or lower highs and lower lows.
## Why this matters
It shows whether the market is trending or shifting direction.
## How it works
A trend changes when the previous structure is broken and a new sequence begins.
> Key concept: A structural shift happens when the market stops making the same type of swing.
## What this looks like in the market
NVDA can make higher highs and higher lows in an uptrend, then shift to lower highs and lower lows.
## Common mistake
Focusing only on the slope of the line and not the sequence of swings.
