# Activity 4.4A — Map a trend shift — find where structure changed direction
## What you're doing
You are tracing the change from one trend structure to another.
## What you need
A chart with a clear trend shift and a way to annotate swings.
## Your task
Mark the highs and lows and identify the point where the structure changed.
## What to look for
Look for the first swing that fails to make the prior trend's required high or low.
