# Module 5 Check
## What you should be able to do now
- Build a setup with confluence from multiple concepts.
- Read context before naming a pattern.
- Explain a full chart read in plain English.
- Recognize when the market is ambiguous and no-trade is the correct choice.
## Quick questions
- What makes confluence stronger than a single signal?
- Why should context come before pattern recognition?
- What is the most important reason to stand aside?
## Chart task
Choose a new chart, write the market context first, identify any setup, and explain why you would trade it or not.
## Before you move on
If you cannot explain your no-trade reasoning clearly, revisit the setup and ambiguity lessons.
