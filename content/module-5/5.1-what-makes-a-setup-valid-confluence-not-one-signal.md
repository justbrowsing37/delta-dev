# What makes a setup valid — confluence, not one signal
## The idea in one sentence
A valid setup is built from multiple signals that support the same idea.
## Why this matters
Relying on a single signal makes trades fragile and easy to defeat.
## How it works
Confluence comes from price structure, liquidity, time frame alignment, and context working together.
> Key concept: The market rewards alignment across factors, not just one pattern.
## What this looks like in the market
A setup may be stronger when a range breakout aligns with a tested level and support from a higher timeframe.
## Common mistake
Treating one signal as enough instead of checking whether other factors agree.
