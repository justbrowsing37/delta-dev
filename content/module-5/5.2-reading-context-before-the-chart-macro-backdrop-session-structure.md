# Reading context before the chart — macro backdrop, session, structure
## The idea in one sentence
Context should come before the chart because it changes how you interpret what you see.
## Why this matters
The same chart pattern can mean very different things in different market environments.
## How it works
Start with the broader market, sector, and session context, then read the chart with that framework.
> Key concept: Context is the lens through which the chart becomes meaningful.
## What this looks like in the market
A setup in a weak market may be less reliable than the same setup in a strong market.
## Common mistake
Jumping straight to a pattern without understanding whether the market mood supports it.
