# Putting a read together — walk through a real example end to end
## The idea in one sentence
A read is the full story of why price is where it is and where it may go next.
## Why this matters
A coherent read separates analysis from guesswork.
## How it works
You combine structure, pattern, liquidity, timeframe context, and invalidation into one clear explanation.
> Key concept: A good read is plain English, not jargon.
## What this looks like in the market
A strong read explains why a setup matters in the current environment, not just that it exists.
## Common mistake
Listing signals without explaining how they connect.
