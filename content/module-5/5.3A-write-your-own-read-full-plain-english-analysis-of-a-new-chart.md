# Activity 5.3A — Write your own read — full plain-English analysis of a new chart
## What you're doing
You are assembling the full analysis of a chart from start to finish.
## What you need
A fresh chart, your notes, and a clear structure for the read.
## Your task
Write what the structure is, what pattern is present, what confirms it, and what would invalidate it.
## What to look for
Emphasize why the setup fits the market context and where the risk points are.
