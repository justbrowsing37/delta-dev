# When to stand aside — recognizing ambiguity and doing nothing
## The idea in one sentence
Sometimes the best decision is to wait because the market is too unclear.
## Why this matters
Active trading does not mean trading every setup.
## How it works
You look for ambiguous structure, conflicting signals, or missing confluence and choose not to trade.
> Key concept: Doing nothing is a valid response to poor conditions.
## What this looks like in the market
A chart with overlapping ranges, mixed trend signals, or no clear liquidity picture is a stand-aside chart.
## Common mistake
Forcing a trade because you feel like you must act.
