# Activity 5.4A — The no-trade journal — find three charts where not trading was right
## What you're doing
You are practicing the discipline of spotting ambiguous setups.
## What you need
Three recent charts and a note-taking format.
## Your task
For each chart, explain why the setup was unclear and why standing aside was the better choice.
## What to look for
Look for mixed signals, weak levels, low liquidity, or context that does not support the idea.
