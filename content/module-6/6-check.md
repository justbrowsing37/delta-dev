# Path selection
## What you should be able to do now
- Describe the three paths and what each one asks of you.
- Match your time availability and goals to one path.
- See the difference between ETF, swing, and intraday trading.
## Quick questions
- What kind of schedule do you want to keep?
- What timeframes do you prefer working with?
- Which path fits your goals best?
## Reflection prompt
Write which path you choose and why, including what you expect from it and what you need to practice.
## Before you move on
If you still feel undecided, review each path and think about the daily commitment it requires.
