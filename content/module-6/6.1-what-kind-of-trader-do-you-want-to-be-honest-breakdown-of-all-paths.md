# What kind of trader do you want to be — honest breakdown of all paths
## The idea in one sentence
Different trading paths require different goals, time, and habits.
## Why this matters
Choosing the right path helps you learn the version of trading that fits you.
## How it works
Each path has its own pace, commitment level, and daily demands.
> Key concept: There is no one best path, only the one that matches your goals and discipline.
## What this looks like in the market
ETF investing, swing trading, and intraday trading all use price understanding, but they use it differently.
## Common mistake
Thinking one path is better than the others instead of thinking about fit.
