# Path A — The long game (ETF, position trading, macro cycles)
## The idea in one sentence
This path focuses on slower moves, bigger timeframes, and steady portfolio building.
## Why this matters
If you want lower frequency and a wider market view, this path fits better.
## How it works
You think in weeks, months, and macro cycles rather than sessions or days.
> Key concept: The long game is about getting the broad market structure right and staying with the best opportunities.
## What this looks like in the market
Positioning into ETFs or index-related themes and holding through normal volatility.
## Common mistake
Expecting daily action from a trade that is meant to unfold over months.
