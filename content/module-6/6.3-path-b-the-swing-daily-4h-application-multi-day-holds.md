# Path B — The swing (daily/4H application, multi-day holds)
## The idea in one sentence
This path is about holding trades for several days to capture medium-term moves.
## Why this matters
It balances time commitment with meaningful price movement.
## How it works
You trade the daily and 4H charts, looking for setups that can run over multiple sessions.
> Key concept: Swing trading is about finding the next leg of a trend or range within a few days.
## What this looks like in the market
A setup that begins on the daily or 4H chart and holds while the market confirms it.
## Common mistake
Treating swing trading like scalping and exiting too early.
