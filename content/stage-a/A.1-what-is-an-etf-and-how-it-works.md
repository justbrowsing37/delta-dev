# What is an ETF and how it works
## The idea in one sentence
An ETF is a fund that owns a basket of assets and trades like a stock.
## Why this matters
ETFs let you get market exposure without picking individual stocks.
## How it works
The fund holds many securities and issues shares that track a market index or theme.
> Key concept: ETFs package diversified exposure in a single tradeable instrument.
## What this looks like in the market
SPY, QQQ, and sector ETFs are examples of funds that can move like the market they track.
## Common mistake
Treating an ETF like a stock without understanding it represents many holdings.
