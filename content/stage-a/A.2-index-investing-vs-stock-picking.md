# Index investing vs stock picking
## The idea in one sentence
Index investing is about broad market exposure, while stock picking is about selecting individual names.
## Why this matters
Your choice changes your risk, research load, and patience required.
## How it works
Index investing spreads risk across many companies; stock picking concentrates it in a few.
> Key concept: The two approaches are different tools, not better or worse labels.
## What this looks like in the market
A broad ETF move can lift many stocks, while a single stock can move independently.
## Common mistake
Assuming index investing is only for buy-and-hold long-term investors.
