# Macro cycles — expansion, contraction, recovery
## The idea in one sentence
The market moves through cycles of growth, pause, and recovery.
## Why this matters
Long-term investors use cycles to understand bigger waves beyond daily noise.
## How it works
Markets expand in strong phases, contract in weak phases, and recover once conditions improve.
> Key concept: Macro cycles are the broad context for long-term positions.
## What this looks like in the market
Index ETFs can rally during expansion and drift during contraction.
## Common mistake
Treating every pullback as a cycle change instead of a normal correction.
