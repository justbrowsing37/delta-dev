# Sector rotation — how money moves between sectors
## The idea in one sentence
Capital shifts between sectors as different parts of the economy become more attractive.
## Why this matters
Knowing the rotation helps you position in the sectors the market favors.
## How it works
Money moves from one sector to another based on growth, risk, and economic conditions.
> Key concept: Sector rotation is a market-wide flow, not a single stock event.
## What this looks like in the market
Technology may lead during expansion while defensive sectors may lead during contraction.
## Common mistake
Chasing the hottest sector after the move is already mature.
