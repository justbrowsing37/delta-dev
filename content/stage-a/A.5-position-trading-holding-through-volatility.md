# Position trading — holding through volatility
## The idea in one sentence
Position trading is staying in a move through normal ups and downs.
## Why this matters
It allows you to capture larger trends while ignoring shorter-term noise.
## How it works
You use higher timeframe levels and broader themes to stay with the dominant move.
> Key concept: Position trading is about the bigger picture, not every daily swing.
## What this looks like in the market
An ETF position may wobble for weeks while the dominant trend remains intact.
## Common mistake
Exiting a position because of a single volatile day instead of looking at the larger trend.
