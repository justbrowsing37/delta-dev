# Building a simple long-term portfolio
## The idea in one sentence
A long-term portfolio blends broad exposures with a clear allocation plan.
## Why this matters
A simple portfolio is easier to manage and less likely to be shaken by short-term swings.
## How it works
You choose a few ETFs or asset classes and assign weights based on your goals.
> Key concept: Consistency is more important than complexity in the long game.
## What this looks like in the market
A long-term portfolio can include a broad market ETF, a sector ETF, and a defensive allocation.
## Common mistake
Overcomplicating the portfolio with too many positions.
