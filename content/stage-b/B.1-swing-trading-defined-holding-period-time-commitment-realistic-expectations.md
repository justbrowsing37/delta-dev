# Swing trading defined — holding period, time commitment, realistic expectations
## The idea in one sentence
Swing trading is holding trades for several days to capture medium-term moves.
## Why this matters
Knowing the timeframe helps you choose the right setups and manage risk.
## How it works
You look for trades that can develop over days while monitoring the daily and 4H charts.
> Key concept: Swing trading is not instant; it requires patience through normal pullbacks.
## What this looks like in the market
A swing trade may take several sessions to reach its target or invalidation.
## Common mistake
Expecting a swing trade to behave like an intraday scalp.
