# Applying Module 4 patterns at the daily level
## The idea in one sentence
The same patterns can work at higher timeframes, but they need stronger confirmation.
## Why this matters
A daily pattern usually carries more weight than a lower-timeframe one.
## How it works
You identify ranges, breakouts, and structure on the daily chart and use them for swing decisions.
> Key concept: Timeframe matters; daily patterns often filter out lower-timeframe noise.
## What this looks like in the market
A daily range breakout on NVDA may be more reliable than a 15-minute breakout.
## Common mistake
Treating a daily setup like a quick session trade.
