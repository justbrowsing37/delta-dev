# Hold management — when to stay in, when to exit early
## The idea in one sentence
Managing a swing position is about deciding whether the trade still has room to work.
## Why this matters
A good entry is only useful if you manage the hold effectively.
## How it works
You watch the setup, the trend, and the price reaction to decide whether to stay or exit.
> Key concept: Exiting early can protect capital, but staying in a valid setup captures more of the move.
## What this looks like in the market
A swing trade may be held through a small pullback if the structure remains intact.
## Common mistake
Giving up on a trade at the first sign of normal volatility.
