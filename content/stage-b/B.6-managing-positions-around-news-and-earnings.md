# Managing positions around news and earnings
## The idea in one sentence
News and earnings can change the expected behavior of a swing trade.
## Why this matters
You need to decide whether the event adds risk or potential reward.
## How it works
You either reduce exposure before the event or avoid new entries into uncertain news.
> Key concept: News is a context factor, not a signal.
## What this looks like in the market
A swing position may be trimmed or held depending on the event and its anticipated impact.
## Common mistake
Treating every news day the same instead of evaluating the specific event.
