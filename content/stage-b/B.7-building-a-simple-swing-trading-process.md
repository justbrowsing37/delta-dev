# Building a simple swing trading process
## The idea in one sentence
A clear process helps you apply the same rules consistently.
## Why this matters
Consistency reduces emotional decisions and keeps your trading disciplined.
## How it works
Your process covers market context, setup selection, risk, and hold management.
> Key concept: The process is more important than any single trade.
## What this looks like in the market
A process might start with a market scan, then move to setup validation and risk placement.
## Common mistake
Having a setup without a repeatable process.
