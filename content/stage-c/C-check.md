# Stage C Check
## What you should be able to do now
- Describe the session-based rhythm of intraday trading.
- Read intraday ranges on 15-minute and 5-minute charts.
- Place entries, stops, and targets for fast trades.
- Recognize the mental discipline needed to avoid overtrading.
## Quick questions
- What is the most important session for liquidity?
- Why does intraday execution require a different stop approach?
- How should you respond when the market is unclear?
## Task
Draft a simple intraday process and explain how you would trade one session.
## Before you move on
If you are unsure about the session structure, revisit the session and intraday range lessons.
