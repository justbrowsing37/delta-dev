# Day trading defined — session commitment, capital requirements, realistic expectations
## The idea in one sentence
Day trading is a session-based approach that requires daily attention and discipline.
## Why this matters
It sets the proper expectations for what this style actually demands.
## How it works
You trade within one session and close positions before the market close.
> Key concept: Day trading is about managing the session, not chasing every move.
## What this looks like in the market
A day trader watches the market open, reacts to the session structure, and closes positions before the end of the day.
## Common mistake
Underestimating the mental and capital demands of intraday decisions.
