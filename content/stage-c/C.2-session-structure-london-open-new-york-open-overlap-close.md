# Session structure — London open, New York open, overlap, close
## The idea in one sentence
The trading day is made of distinct sessions with different activity levels.
## Why this matters
Knowing the session structure helps you place trades when liquidity and momentum are strongest.
## How it works
Different regions open at different times, and the overlaps often create the biggest moves.
> Key concept: The session rhythm gives a framework for where intraday volume and volatility concentrate.
## What this looks like in the market
The New York open and the overlap with London often produce the most meaningful intraday moves.
## Common mistake
Trading without respect for the session cycle and its impact on liquidity.
