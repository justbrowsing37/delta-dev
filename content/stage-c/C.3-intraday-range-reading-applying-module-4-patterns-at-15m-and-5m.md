# Intraday range reading — applying Module 4 patterns at 15M and 5M
## The idea in one sentence
The same range and breakout patterns can be used on short intraday timeframes.
## Why this matters
It allows you to read the session structure while staying within the intraday context.
## How it works
You draw ranges and levels on 15-minute and 5-minute charts and watch how price behaves around them.
> Key concept: Shorter timeframes still have structure; the patterns are just faster.
## What this looks like in the market
An intraday range on the 15-minute chart can define the acceptable move for the session.
## Common mistake
Treating every small move as a new trend instead of part of an intraday range.
