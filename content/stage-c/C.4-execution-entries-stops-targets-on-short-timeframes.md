# Execution — entries, stops, targets on short timeframes
## The idea in one sentence
Execution discipline is critical when trading fast intraday moves.
## Why this matters
The right entry and stop on a short timeframe can make the difference between a good trade and a big loss.
## How it works
You size for the timeframe, place stops around clean structure, and set targets that fit the session range.
> Key concept: Execution is more important than picking the perfect pattern.
## What this looks like in the market
An intraday entry may be at a level tested during the opening range with a tight but fair stop.
## Common mistake
Using a stop that is too tight for the intraday swing or too wide for the time horizon.
