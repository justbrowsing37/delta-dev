# The mental demands of intraday trading — patience, discipline, not overtrading
## The idea in one sentence
Day trading requires emotional control and the ability to wait for the right moments.
## Why this matters
Without discipline, the speed of intraday markets creates costly mistakes.
## How it works
You manage your attention, avoid chasing noise, and stick to your plan.
> Key concept: The biggest edge in intraday trading is often mental, not technical.
## What this looks like in the market
A disciplined trader may sit through long quiet periods and act only when the session gives a clear edge.
## Common mistake
Overtrading just because the market is moving.
