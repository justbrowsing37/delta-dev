# Common intraday mistakes and how to avoid them
## The idea in one sentence
Knowing the common errors helps you avoid the most damaging intraday traps.
## Why this matters
The same mistakes repeat for many traders; learning them saves time and capital.
## How it works
You identify the traps, like chasing, revenge trading, and bad execution, and adopt rules to prevent them.
> Key concept: The best defense is a clear process and a rule-based approach.
## What this looks like in the market
Mistakes show up as wide stops, overleveraged entries, or holding through clear invalidation.
## Common mistake
Blaming the market instead of the process when a trade fails.
