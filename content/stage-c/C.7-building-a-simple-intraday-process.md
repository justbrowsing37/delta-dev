# Building a simple intraday process
## The idea in one sentence
A repeatable process keeps intraday trading manageable and disciplined.
## Why this matters
A clear process prevents emotional decisions in a fast-moving market.
## How it works
You define when you trade, how you size, where you enter, and when you exit.
> Key concept: The process should fit the session and your available time.
## What this looks like in the market
A process might start with pre-market context, then an opening range plan and specific entry criteria.
## Common mistake
Trading without a written process or without reviewing it before the session.
