# The full trading landscape — holding periods, asset classes, the full map
## The idea in one sentence
Trading fits into a landscape of different asset classes and holding periods.
## Why this matters
Knowing the map helps you understand where your chosen path sits.
## How it works
Different styles use different timeframes, instruments, and rules.
> Key concept: No single market or holding period is inherently better than another.
## What this looks like in the market
Stocks, futures, forex, and crypto all exist on different parts of the trading landscape.
## Common mistake
Thinking that day trading or investing is the only valid way to trade.
