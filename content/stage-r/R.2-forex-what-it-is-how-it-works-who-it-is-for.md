# Forex — what it is, how it works, who it is for
## The idea in one sentence
Forex is the market for trading currencies against each other.
## Why this matters
It is one of the largest and most liquid markets, with its own rules.
## How it works
You trade currency pairs, and the price reflects the relative value of two currencies.
> Key concept: Forex is about global macro and interest rate differences, not company profits.
## What this looks like in the market
Pairs like EUR/USD and USD/JPY move on economic news and central bank policy.
## Common mistake
Treating forex like a stock market instead of a currency market.
