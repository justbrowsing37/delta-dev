# Options — rights, obligations, and why complexity matters
## The idea in one sentence
Options give you the right to buy or sell an asset, but they also involve obligations and time decay.
## Why this matters
Options add complexity and risk, so they require a clear purpose.
## How it works
Call options allow you to buy, put options allow you to sell, and their value changes with price, volatility, and time.
> Key concept: Options are not a shortcut; they are a different instrument with its own risks.
## What this looks like in the market
Options are used for hedging, income, and directional strategies.
## Common mistake
Trading options without understanding the impact of theta and gamma.
