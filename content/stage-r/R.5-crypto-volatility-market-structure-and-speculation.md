# Crypto — volatility, market structure, and speculation
## The idea in one sentence
Crypto is a highly volatile market with unique structure and speculative behavior.
## Why this matters
It behaves differently from traditional stocks and requires extra caution.
## How it works
Crypto prices move on sentiment, adoption, and liquidity, often with large gaps and fast moves.
> Key concept: Crypto is a speculative market, not a substitute for established trading paths.
## What this looks like in the market
Bitcoin and Ethereum can swing sharply on headlines and on-chain flows.
## Common mistake
Assuming crypto is the same as stock trading because the charts look similar.
