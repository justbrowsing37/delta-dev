# Tools and platforms worth knowing — charting, brokers, screeners
## The idea in one sentence
The right tools help you read the market and manage your process.
## Why this matters
Good tools make analysis faster and clearer, but they do not replace your judgment.
## How it works
Charting platforms show price and structure; brokers connect you to execution; screeners help you find opportunities.
> Key concept: Tools are support, not the source of your trading edge.
## What this looks like in the market
Common tools include charting platforms, order execution brokers, and news or scan services.
## Common mistake
Believing tool quality is more important than your own market understanding.
