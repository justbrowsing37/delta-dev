# Glossary — living document, all terms used across the curriculum
## The idea in one sentence
A glossary keeps the vocabulary consistent and easy to reference.
## Why this matters
Clear definitions prevent confusion over the same terms used in different lessons.
## How it works
You collect the key terms from the curriculum and define them in simple language.
> Key concept: A glossary is a living tool that grows as the curriculum grows.
## What this looks like in the market
Terms like support, resistance, breakout, and liquidity each get a clear definition.
## Common mistake
Using jargon without a shared definition.
